How the Model Works
What Give Props AI actually does
The model predicts how many strikeouts, walks, and hits a pitcher will allow in a given start. It compares that prediction to the sportsbook line — if the model is confident the line is wrong and the pitcher has the right profile, it flags a pick.
Data Sources
Statcast pitch-by-pitch data
Batter K-rate by lineup slot
Umpire tendency data
3-year pitcher history
Live odds from 10+ books
Pitcher Archetypes
Every pitcher classified by:
K archetype (POWER / AVG / CONTACT)
BB archetype (ELITE_CMD / CONTROL / VOLATILE)
GB profile (GROUNDBALL / FLYBALL)
Edge Detection
Model prediction vs consensus line
Confidence score (model certainty)
Experience multiplier (MLB track record)
Only picks with conf ≥ 0.35 + exp ≥ 0.97 qualify
V2 Filter Rules — Why We're Selective
The V2 filter exists because not all model picks are equal. Early tracking (Mar 26–Apr 4) showed raw model picks at 58–62%. Applying filters improved forward results by removing structurally weak pick types.
Away Strikeouts Only
Home K unders hit at 42% vs 75% for away. Home pitchers face pitch count limits and crowd disruption. Single biggest filter improvement.
HIGH_K Arm Exclusion
Elite strikeout pitchers at low K lines — books price these correctly. Model edge disappears for these matchups.
VOLATILE_BB + AVERAGE_BB Exclusion
Pitchers who average 1.5–2+ walks per start excluded from walk bets. WILD_EFFECTIVE 1–3 (−61% ROI) in forward test confirmed this.
p_under ≥ 0.55 (Walks) — added Apr 9
Raw model conviction must be ≥ 55% for walk bets. Prevents the experience multiplier from inflating borderline picks past the confidence threshold.
Confidence ≥ 0.35
Model calibration score must clear 0.35 — ensures genuine confidence in the line mismatch, not just marginal above fair value.
Experience ≥ 0.97
Pitcher must have sufficient MLB track record for features to be reliable. Rookies and limited-sample pitchers are excluded from core picks.
Closing Line Value — Why It Matters
CLV proves edge is real, independent of outcomes
If the market moves the line in the direction of our picks before game time, sharp money agreed with us. Consistent positive CLV = the model finds real information, not just getting lucky on results. We capture closing lines automatically on every pick.
